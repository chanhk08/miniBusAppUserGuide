Getting Started Guide

The Minibus Mobile App simplifies commuting on Hong Kong’s green minibuses with features like QR code boarding, drop-off requests, and location sharing. Designed for locals, tourists, and passengers with special needs, it offers a multilingual, user-friendly interface. This guide walks you through setting up and using the app’s core features.

Note: The app requires internet and location services for full functionality. During peak hours or in areas with poor signal, features like QR code scanning may experience delays. Ensure your device has sufficient battery.

1. Navigating the Home Page

The Home Page is your hub for viewing minibus routes and accessing features.

  1. Open the app to view the Home Page.
  2. Check the nearest route and its distance (e.g., “Clear Water Bay Road, 0.74 km away”).
  3. Note the estimated time for the next minibus (e.g., “Next: 21 min”).
  4. Use the bottom navigation bar to switch to Home, Bookmark, or Settings.

Accessibility: Enable audio descriptions for route details. Multilingual route names assist tourists.

[Screenshot: Home Page with route details and navigation bar.]

2. Logging In

Log in to access restricted features like QR code boarding.

  1. Launch the app and attempt to access a restricted feature (e.g., QR Code).
  2. When prompted with “Login Required,” tap “Login.”
  3. Enter your registered email (e.g., “john@example.com”) and password.
  4. Tap “Login” to continue.

Accessibility: Screen readers can guide visually impaired users through the login form. Multilingual prompts support non-local users.

[Screenshot: Login screen with email and password fields.]

3. Using QR Code for Boarding

Board a minibus by scanning a QR code, ideal for seamless commuting.

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. From the Home Page, tap “QR Code.”
  3. Allow camera access when prompted.
  4. Scan the QR code inside the minibus (e.g., for “Yau Yat Tsuen to Shek Kip Mei Station, Route ID: 2005687”).
  5. Verify the route details (origin and destination) on the screen.
  6. Tap “Enter The Bus” to confirm boarding.

Accessibility: This feature supports mute or speech-impaired users by enabling digital boarding. Screen readers confirm route details.

[Screenshot: QR code scanning and route verification screen.]

4. Requesting a Drop-Off

Request a drop-off without verbal communication while on the minibus.

  1. While on the minibus, access the “QR Code” section.
  2. View the current route details (e.g., “41A, 23 Sha On St, Ma On Shan”).
  3. Tap the “Drop Off” button to request a stop.
  4. After processing, see “Thank you for riding with us!” and tap “Back to Home.”

Accessibility: Silent drop-off requests aid mute or speech-impaired users. Multilingual prompts assist tourists.

Note: If you scan another QR code while on a bus, the app will display: “You are already on a bus. Please get off before scanning another QR code.”

[Screenshot: Drop-off request screen with route details.]

5. Managing Settings

Customize your app experience through the Settings page.

  1. From the Home Page, tap “Settings” in the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Explore options: Notifications, Language, Help Center, About Us, Emergency Contact.
  3. To adjust notifications, tap “Notification” and enable/disable as needed.
  4. To change language, tap “Change the display languages” and select your preference (e.g., English, Chinese).
  5. Access the Help Center or About Us for support or app details.
  6. To log out, tap “Logout” and confirm.

Accessibility: Screen readers describe settings options. Multilingual support ensures ease for all users.

[Screenshot: Settings page with options and language selection.]

6. Managing Emergency Contacts

Add, edit, or delete emergency contacts for safety notifications.

  • Adding a Contact:
    1. From Settings, tap “Emergency Contacts.”
    2. Tap “Add contact.”
    3. Enter the name (optional), country code (e.g., “+852”), and phone number (e.g., “999”).
    4. Tap “Save.”
    5. See “New contact added successfully” and tap “OK.”
  • Editing a Contact:
    1. In Emergency Contacts, tap “Edit” next to a contact (e.g., “Po, +852999”).
    2. Update name, country code, or phone number.
    3. Tap “Save” or “Cancel.”
  • Deleting a Contact:
    1. Tap “Delete” next to a contact.
    2. Confirm by tapping “Delete” in the prompt or “Cancel” to keep it.

Accessibility: Screen readers guide users through contact management. Voice dictation can assist with entering details.

[Screenshot: Emergency Contacts page with add/edit/delete options.]

7. Monitoring and Sharing Location

Monitor your location and share it with emergency contacts for safety.

  1. Enable location services when prompted on the Home Page.
  2. View your current location and nearby routes.
  3. Ensure emergency contacts are added in Settings.
  4. In an emergency, the app automatically shares your location with contacts if permissions are granted.

Accessibility: Audio descriptions can announce location updates. Multilingual prompts guide setup.

[Screenshot: Home Page with location enabled and nearby routes.]

8. Providing Feedback

Share feedback to improve the app experience.

  1. From the Home Page or Settings, navigate to the Feedback section.
  2. Read guidelines recommending travel date, time, boarding location, and vehicle details.
  3. Tap “Got it” to proceed.
  4. Enter Route Number (e.g., “2A”) and Vehicle Number (e.g., “AB1234”).
  5. Optionally, add travel date/time (e.g., “July 5th, 09:15-09:30”) and boarding location (e.g., “Rainbow Village”).
  6. Tap “Next” to submit.

Accessibility: Screen readers read guidelines and form fields. Voice dictation can assist with input.

[Screenshot: Feedback form with route and vehicle fields.]

9. Basic Troubleshooting

Resolve common issues for a smooth experience.

  • QR Code Scanning Issues: Ensure camera permissions are granted. If you see “You are already on a bus,” request a drop-off before scanning another QR code.
  • Location Services Errors: Enable location services in your device settings. In areas with poor signal, updates may delay.
  • Login Failures: Verify your email and password. Use “Forgot Password” to reset if needed.
  • Battery Drain: Continuous location tracking may increase battery usage. Lower screen brightness or disable tracking when not needed.
  • Need Help? Access the Help Center in Settings or contact support via the “Contact” section.

Note: For persistent issues, use the Emergency Contact feature for urgent support.
